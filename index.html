The Man Behind the Heroes

Stanley Martin Lieber or better known as Stan Lee was an amazing comic book artist responsible for most of your favorite comic book superheroes that come from Marvel Comics. Some of these heroes, you no doubtably already know: Spider-Man, Hulk, Thor, and even Iron man. He was a truly innovative force in the world of pop culture in a time where things like comic books were at a bit of a low.
